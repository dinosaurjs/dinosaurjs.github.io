What is Dinosaur.js?

A non-profit, community-driven JavaScript conference happening in Denver, Colorado on Friday, June 24, 2016. One day. One track.

Dinosaur.js is part of the JSConf Family of Events.

What’s in a Name?

So, why is it even called Dinosaur.js? Good question. Dinosaur Ridge is just outside of Denver. It’s where the Apatosaurus, Diplodocus, Stegosaurus, and Allosaurus (née Brontosaurus) were first found.
